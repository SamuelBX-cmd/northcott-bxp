Principle 02
Known,
not just served
Continuity that builds real trust
People don't want to be a case number. Northcott earns loyalty by treating every person as a whole — across time, services, and transitions.
  • No retelling the story — continuity across services
  • Proactive communication before people ask
  • Warm, consistent handoffs between teams
Good Services: "Consistent throughout" · "Works in a way that is familiar"
02
Principle 03
Confidence
in complexity
Expert clarity, not expert jargon
The NDIS is genuinely hard. Northcott's job is to make people feel capable — not grateful for being handled.
  • Plain language at every stage, every channel
  • Content that builds knowledge, not dependency
  • Staff who guide with expertise and warmth
Good Services: "Clearly explains why a decision was made" · "Is findable"
03
Principle 04
Part of something
bigger
Inclusion as action, not aspiration
Northcott's purpose is a more inclusive society — felt in every interaction, not just announced. People leave knowing they matter beyond the transaction.
  • Celebrate what matters to people, not just outputs
  • Community presence that creates belonging
  • Advocate visibly, not just internally
Good Services: "Encourages appropriate behaviours" · "Is not too narrow in scope"
04
These are not values on a wall. They are standards for behaviour — a shared language for how every person at Northcott shows up, every day.
